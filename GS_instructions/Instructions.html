FRC 2018 Game Simulator



P1: scroll down with LEFT STICK


RULES:


Scoring:

OWNERSHIP is achieved when the circle icon is your ALLIANCE's color and tiped towards your ALLIANCE's side of that field element.


Power Ups:


Each Power Up requires 3 POWER CUBES in that section of the VALUT to activate.
Power Ups activate when the 3rd POWER CUBE is placed.
If another Power Up is already active, the Power Up will activate after the previous one expires.


End Game:


Must reach 40 in order for your climb to count.
